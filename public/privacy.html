Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Radulator ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website radulator.com (the "Site"), which provides free, evidence-based medical calculators for healthcare professionals and medical learners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Site.

By using radulator.com, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Site.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you submit our website feedback form (powered by Formspree), we may collect the following information. This form is used solely to gather feedback for improving the Site and is not used to collect any clinical or patient data:

  • Name (required) — to identify the feedback source
  • Email (required) — to respond to your feedback
  • Feedback Type (required) — Bug Report, Feature Request, or General Feedback
  • Calculator (optional) — to identify the relevant calculator
  • Message (required) — to receive your detailed feedback

Important: The feedback form is used solely to collect user suggestions, bug reports, and general feedback to improve the website. We do not collect any patient data, protected health information (PHI), or clinical data through the Site. Our calculators operate entirely in your browser and do not transmit calculation inputs or results to our servers.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymized usage data. The following events are tracked:

  • Calculator Selected: Which calculator a user opens
  • Calculation Performed: When a calculation is run (not the inputs or results)
  • Calculator Result Viewed: When results are displayed
  • File Download: When a file is downloaded
  • Outbound Link Click: When an external link is clicked
  • Feedback Submitted: When the feedback form is submitted
  • Search Queries: Calculator search terms entered in the search box
  • Reference Link Clicks: Which external reference links you click from calculator pages

Google Analytics may also collect standard web analytics data such as your IP address (anonymized), browser type, device type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This data is processed by Google under its own privacy policy.

2.3 Cookies and Local Storage

Radulator itself does not set cookies or use browser local storage. However, Google Analytics uses cookies (primarily _ga and _gid) to distinguish unique visitors. You can control cookies through your browser settings or by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your feedback and improve our calculators
  • To understand how users interact with our Site and identify areas for improvement
  • To monitor and analyze usage trends and aggregate statistics
  • To fix bugs, improve performance, and develop new features
  • To display relevant advertisements (if applicable)
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4 — Website analytics and usage tracking. Privacy Policy
  • Formspree — Feedback form submission processing. Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting. Privacy Policy

Advertising

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense or similar services. These services may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Each of these services may collect additional information as described in their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Data Retention

Feedback form data (name, email, message) is retained in Formspree for as long as necessary to respond to your feedback and improve the website. This data is used exclusively for website improvement purposes.

Google Analytics data is retained according to Google's default retention settings (14 months for user-level data). You may request deletion of your personal data at any time (see Section 8).

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Our Site is served over HTTPS, encrypting data in transit
  • We use reputable third-party service providers with their own security measures
  • Our codebase is maintained in a private repository
  • All calculators process data client-side only — no calculation data is transmitted to any server

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Radulator is intended for use by healthcare professionals and medical learners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

8.1 For All Users

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Use Google's opt-out browser add-on

8.2 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights

8.3 European Economic Area Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, our legal basis for processing your data is legitimate interest (for analytics) and consent (for feedback form submissions). You have additional rights including data portability, the right to restrict processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

9. Do Not Track Signals

We do not currently respond to Do Not Track browser signals. However, you can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using Google's opt-out browser add-on.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside your state, province, or country where data protection laws may differ. Google Analytics and Formspree process data on servers in the United States. By using the Site, you consent to such transfers.

11. Medical Information Disclaimer

Radulator provides medical calculators for educational and informational purposes only. The calculators are not a substitute for professional medical judgment, diagnosis, or treatment. We do not collect, store, or process any patient health information through our calculators. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.

All calculator inputs and results, including patient-reported outcome scores, are processed entirely within your browser. No patient data, clinical values, or questionnaire responses are transmitted to, stored on, or accessible by our servers. When you close or refresh the page, all entered data is permanently discarded. CSV exports are generated locally in your browser and downloaded directly to your device.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to exercise your privacy rights, or need to report a concern, please contact us through our feedback form.
