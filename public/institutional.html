Radulator Institutional

A controlled static-build architecture for browser-local medical calculator reference releases.

Current Status

The first institutional implementation uses an empty calculator allowlist. No calculator is included until release-specific rights, validation, owner review, and deployment approval are complete.

Implemented Controls

  • Browser-local app execution with no calculator input or result transmission.
  • No telemetry, feedback form, advertising, tracking cookie, remote font, third-party script, CDN script, or off-origin automatic request in the institutional build.
  • Versioned release metadata, calculator allowlist hashes, validation-manifest hashes, rollback documentation, and first-party emergency release control.
  • No EHR, PACS, Epic, API, PHI, or institutional-network integration.
  • Independent professional review remains required; the app does not replace professional judgment.
  • Accessibility, security, incident-response, vulnerability disclosure, and SBOM processes are documented for release review.

Evidence Boundary

Validation, licensing status, and institutional eligibility vary by calculator and by release. Engineering proof covers the implemented build controls; medical, rights, deployment, and regulatory decisions remain explicit owner or counsel review items.
